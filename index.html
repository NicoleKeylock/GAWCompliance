Post-Brexit · FSA
🇬🇧
United Kingdom
Governed by the FSA and Wine Regulations 2011 as amended. Key FBO/importer labelling changes from 1 January 2024. Nutrition labelling not yet legally mandatory but under active consultation.
📋 FSA Wine Labelling 🏛️ GOV.UK Wine Import 🍺 Portman Group 💧 Drinkaware Licence
📋 Mandatory Label Elements
Product Category
Mandatory
Omit if PDO/PGI designation shown (e.g. Rioja satisfies this). Common misunderstanding — check before flagging.
GAW standard: RED WINE · WHITE WINE · ROSÉ WINE · SPARKLING WINE (all caps)
Country of Origin
Mandatory
Always mandatory. For blended wines from multiple countries, all must be named.
GAW standard: PRODUCT OF [COUNTRY]
Net Volume
Mandatory
ml and cl both legally acceptable. Flag cl as advisory on GAW own brand labels for consistency.
GAW standard: 750ml (lowercase ml, no space)
ABV Declaration
Mandatory
Tolerance: ±0.5% up to 5.5% ABV, ±1% above 5.5%. Must match analysis.
GAW standard: 13% vol. — space between % and vol, full stop
FBO / Distributor
MandatoryFrom Jan 2024
UK/CI/IoM based business name and address. "Importer" wording no longer required. Not needed on retailer own-labels.
GAW standard: DISTRIBUTED BY GAW TA14 6QR, UK
Error to flag: "SELECTED BY" — must be corrected
Lot Number
Mandatory
L-prefix Julian format. Can be on label or bottle — GAW preference is both.
Format: L + 2-digit year + 3-digit day + batch e.g. L18065D01
⚠️ Allergens
Sulphites
Mandatory
If present above 10mg/L — applies to virtually all wines.
GAW UK standard: Allergy advice: Contains Sulphites
"Allergy advice:" bold · "Contains" regular · allergen name bold
Egg & Milk (fining agents)
If present
Albumin (egg) and casein (milk) must be declared if residues exceed 0.25mg/L. Confirm via spec sheet.
Example: Allergy advice: Contains Sulphites, Egg
♻️ OPRL Recycling
OPRL Statement
Mandatory
On Pack Recycling Label must be accurate for the closure type. Common error: generic "Recycle" used when closure requires specific instruction.
Screwcap: CAP ON — Recycle (or CAP OFF depending on type)
Cork: Check local authority guidance
Common error: Label says "Recycle" but closure needs "CAP OFF — Recycle"
Glass Bottle
Mandatory
Glass bottle recycling statement must be present and accurate.
Standard: Widely Recycled — Glass Bottle
Verify OPRL statement matches spec closure type — flag any mismatch as critical
🍺 Responsible Drinking
Pregnant Woman Symbol
Mandatory
Crossed-out pregnant woman symbol must appear on all UK wine labels.
Units of Alcohol
Best PracticeGAW Standard
Formula: ABV% × Volume(ml) ÷ 1000. Rounded to one decimal place.
Example: 13 × 750 ÷ 1000 = 9.8 units
Drinkaware Statement
Best PracticeGAW Standard
Requires active GAW Drinkaware licence. Ireland line only when Republic of Ireland is a target market.
PLEASE DRINK RESPONSIBLY
For more information visit bedrinkaware.co.uk
or in the Republic of Ireland, visit drinkaware.ie
CMO Guidelines
Best Practice
Chief Medical Officers' low risk guidelines. Wording is prescribed — no deviation. Usually retailer-specified format.
"The UK Chief Medical Officers recommend adults do not regularly drink more than 14 units a week."
📝 GAW Internal Notes
✏️ UK Notes
• "SELECTED BY" is a legacy error — always correct to "DISTRIBUTED BY GAW TA14 6QR, UK" • Retailer own-labels do not carry GAW distributor line • Confirm GAW Drinkaware licence is active before any UK label goes to print • PDO/PGI designation satisfies wine category requirement — team sometimes adds unnecessary descriptor • OPRL statement must match the closure type on the spec — CAP ON/OFF confusion is common • QR code increasingly retailer-expected even though not yet legally required
Reg. 2021/2117
🇪🇺
European Union
Regulation (EU) 2021/2117 introduced mandatory nutrition and ingredient labelling from 8 December 2023 for wines from the 2024 harvest onwards. Pre-2023 vintage wines may be sold until stocks exhausted.
⚖️ Regulation 2021/2117 🇪🇺 EC Wine Labelling 📋 Delegated Reg. 2019/934
📋 Mandatory Elements
Product Category
Mandatory
Omit if PDO/PGI shown. Rioja, Rías Baixas, Crémant etc. all satisfy this requirement.
GAW standard: RED WINE · WHITE WINE (all caps)
Country of Origin
Mandatory
"Product of EU" not sufficient — specific country required.
GAW standard: PRODUCT OF [COUNTRY]
Net Volume
Mandatory
ml and cl legally acceptable. Flag cl as advisory on GAW own brand.
GAW standard: 750ml
ABV Declaration
Mandatory
±0.5% tolerance. Specified in % vol.
GAW standard: 13% vol. (space between % and vol)
Bottler / Distributor
Mandatory
Bottler name and address plus GAW distributor line. Dual-market labels use both French and English.
French: MIS EN BOUTEILLE PAR [PRODUCER] [REG] DISTRIBUÉ PAR GAW TA14 6QR, UK
English: BOTTLED BY [PRODUCER] [REG] DISTRIBUTED BY GAW TA14 6QR, UK
Pregnant Woman Symbol
Mandatory
Mandatory on all EU wine labels.
Sweetness Term (Sparkling)
Sparkling only
Mandatory for ALL EU sparkling wines — not just Champagne. Applies to Crémant, Cava, Prosecco, Sekt. May be on front label.
Terms: Brut Nature · Extra Brut · Brut · Extra Dry · Dry · Demi-Sec · Doux
Lot Number
Mandatory
L-prefix Julian format. GAW preference: on both bottle neck and label.
Format: L18065D01 (L + year + Julian day + batch ref)
🧪 Nutrition & Ingredients (2024 harvest+)
Energy Declaration
MandatoryDec 2023
Must be on physical label. Per 100ml in kJ and kcal. E symbol. Whole numbers only.
GAW standard: E (100ml) : 277kJ / 66kcal
E bold · rest regular · kJ (lowercase k, uppercase J) · kcal all lowercase
INGREDIENTS & NUTRITION Block
MandatoryDec 2023
GAW uses Bottlebooks. Unique QR per SKU — new QR required per vintage. No marketing content, no user tracking on linked page.
INGREDIENTS & NUTRITION
[QR code — min 13×13mm recommended]
E (100ml) : XkJ / Xkcal
⚠️ Allergens
Multi-Language Allergen String
Mandatory
Must appear on physical label in languages of all destination markets. GAW dual-market standard covers 6 languages in this order:
CONTIENT DES SULFITES / CONTAINS SULPHITES / BEVAT SULFIETEN / ENTHÄLT SULFITE / INDEHOLDER SULFITTER / CONTIENE SULFITOS
French · English · Dutch · German · Danish · Spanish

Add egg/milk translations if applicable to spec
🖨️ Print & Format
Minimum Font Size
Mandatory
All mandatory information minimum 1.2mm x-height (height of lowercase letter).
Print reminder: Confirm with artwork supplier before print approval
Field of Vision
Mandatory
All mandatory info visible without rotating bottle. Exception: allergens and lot number may appear elsewhere.
OPRL / Recycling
Producer responsibility
Recycling icons are the producer's responsibility. Italy and France have mandatory requirements.
Italy: Decreto 116/2020 — mandatory recycling icons
France: Triman logo required
Vintage Year
GAW Preference
GAW preference is NOT to show vintage on front label where possible — keeps label usable across vintages without reprinting.
📝 GAW Internal Notes
✏️ EU Notes
• New vintage = new Bottlebooks QR code entry required — never reuse previous vintage QR • Energy declaration (kJ/kcal) must be verified as updated for new vintage — calories change with ABV/sugar changes • PDO/PGI designation satisfies wine category requirement • "SELECTED BY" must always be corrected to "DISTRIBUTED BY" • Post-Brexit: common back label now achievable for UK+EU due to FBO labelling change • Italy: flag mandatory recycling icons separately when Italy is a target market

📋 Known Requirements (verify)
COLA Approval
Mandatory
Every wine label must receive TTB Certificate of Label Approval before sale. Each label change requires a new COLA.
Health Warning Statement
Mandatory
Prescribed US Government health warning — exact wording, no variation permitted.
"GOVERNMENT WARNING: (1) According to the Surgeon General, women should not drink alcoholic beverages during pregnancy because of the risk of birth defects. (2) Consumption of alcoholic beverages impairs your ability to drive a car or operate machinery, and may cause health problems."
Importer Details
Mandatory
Name and address of US importer must appear.
GAW example: IMPORTED BY: [CUSTOMER], [CITY], [STATE]
Net Contents
Mandatory
Must be declared in metric (ml) for wines over 187ml.

Aldi UK + Ireland
Retailer own-label profile. All design files come from Aldi's team. Dimensions, Pantones, paper type, varnish and style guide are specified by Aldi and contained in the design brief. GAW checks compliance and sends approved artwork to the winery.
ℹ️
Workflow reminder: Stage 1 = check Aldi design file for compliance. Stage 2 = check printer proof against Stage 1 approved file (like-for-like only).
🏷️ Aldi Fixed Requirements (every label)
Volume Format
Aldi standard is 75cl — do NOT flag as non-GAW-standard.
Required: 75cl (not 750ml)
Nutrition — Per 100ml + Per 125ml Glass
Both per 100ml AND per 125ml glass (125ml = standard Aldi serving) required.
Example: Energy: Per 100ml: 319kJ/76kcal · Per glass (125ml): 399kJ/96kcal
Servings Declaration
Number of servings per bottle must be declared (6 servings for 75cl).
Standard: "This bottle contains 6 servings"
Style Guide Scale
Light Bodied → Full Bodied scale required with position marker. Comes from Aldi design file and spec.
Flavours & Pair With
Flavour descriptor and food pairing sections required on Aldi back labels.
CMO Guidelines
Full CMO wording required on all Aldi labels.
"The UK Chief Medical Officers recommend adults do not regularly drink more than 14 units per week."
Drinkaware — Both URLs
Both .co.uk and .ie URLs required on Aldi UK+Ireland shared labels.
PLEASE DRINK RESPONSIBLY
For more information visit bedrinkaware.co.uk
or in the Republic of Ireland, visit drinkaware.ie
Satisfaction Guarantee
Fixed Aldi wording — must match exactly.
"If you are not entirely satisfied with this product please return it to the store where it was purchased and we will be pleased to replace or refund it. This does not affect your statutory rights."
SPECIALLY PRODUCED FOR
Fixed wording with both Aldi addresses. Never changes between products.
SPECIALLY PRODUCED FOR: Aldi Stores Ltd, PO Box 26, Atherstone, Warwickshire CV9 2SH / Aldi Stores (Ireland) Ltd, PO Box 726, Naas, Co. Kildare. www.aldi.ie
QR Code — Aldi Hosted
Aldi supply and host their own QR code. GAW does not use Bottlebooks for Aldi labels.
Required wording near QR: "Scan for Ingredients and Nutrition"
On-Pack Recycle Logo
OPRL logo must be present and accurate for closure type. Check against spec.
Common error: Generic "Recycle" used when closure needs "CAP OFF — Recycle" or "CAP ON"
Aldi Barcode
Barcode is supplied by Aldi — verify it matches what Aldi provided.
Bottled By Statement
Producer name and address must appear. Aldi format uses "Bottled by:" not GAW distributor line.
Allergy Advice Format
Must follow GAW UK standard format.
Allergy advice: Contains Sulphites
Vegan Logo
Only include if wine is certified vegan per spec. Flag if present on non-vegan wine or missing on vegan wine.
Lot Code on Label
Lot code visible on Aldi labels (confirmed from approved artwork). Julian format.
📐 Specification Notes
✏️ Aldi Notes
• Dimensions, Pantones, paper type and varnish all come from Aldi's design brief — not GAW's responsibility to specify • Aldi supply and host their own QR code — GAW does not set up Bottlebooks for Aldi • FOP (Baron Amarillo example): 118×74mm · Manter Sorolla paper · Pantone Black 6U, 122U, 3546U · Estampacion Kurz 457 foil · Barniz Antirayaduras varnish · Position 40mm from base • BOP (Baron Amarillo example): 95×75mm · Sorolla H+O Ultra WS · Pantone 440U · Barniz Antirayaduras · Position 58mm from base • Printer: Grupo Argraf, Oyon, Alava, Spain (for Baron Amarillo — may vary by product) • Aldi Ireland and UK share the same label — both Drinkaware URLs always required
